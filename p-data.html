Environmental Data-Driven Display

Visit Process book

Role - Data Analysis, User Research, Design

In the project, I first analyzed a set of data about a fictional transport hub with data about planes, trains, and ferries. I then modeled it with diagrams to describe cardinality and temporality that could be meaningful to the users.

At the second step, I reviewed personas and use cases of stakeholders and users of the public display, and identify the data that would best satisfy their correspoding needs.

With an understading of the stakes of the data, I then made hand sketches to generate ideas for the display. Having incorporated feedback from critics of my peers and my instructor, I made 4 rounds of digital sketch with added animations to reach the final display.
