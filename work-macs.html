Graphic Design:
Mobile App Compliance System

Learn More

Role - Graphic design, UX / UI, Branding

The Mobile App Compliance System helps users to tell whether an app behaves as its privacy policy promises. It is a scalable system that analyzes Android apps compliance with privacy requirements.
As a graphic designer, I work closely with the lab in the Institute of Software Research at Carnegie Mellon University to come up with logo and banner design, as well as branding.
